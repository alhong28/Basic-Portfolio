About Me



Thinker. Artist. Dude. I am all of these things some of the time...at least I'd like to think so. I graduated from Rutgers 2014 with a Master's Degree. My academic from diverse areas led me to Human Resources. After some skin in the game, I wanted to do something more, something more creative. Then in one decisive swoop, I ended up in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp and as happy as can be learning.

I'm livid in creating. Ever since I was a young lad, I loved to draw and make designs out of lego. This passion for creation combined with technology opens so many doors and applications. Other passions of mine are films, hiking, kayaking, and traveling.
